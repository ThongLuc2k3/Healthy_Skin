# WS10.2 - Cau hoi phan bien va goi y tra loi

## San pham va van de

1. Vi sao DA DUONG giai quyet cung luc ca cham soc da va dinh duong?
Goi y: Vi nhom nhin van de tu goc do co dia va thoi quen song; hai quyet dinh nay thuong tach roi tren thi truong nhung lai lien quan den cung nguoi dung.

2. Diem khac biet lon nhat cua du an so voi app skincare thong thuong la gi?
Goi y: Ho so dung chung cho da va dinh duong, co roadmap va check-in de duy tri hanh vi, khong chi la tra ket qua mot lan.

3. Du an nay la cong cu y te hay cong cu ho tro ra quyet dinh?
Goi y: Day la cong cu ho tro ra quyet dinh va giao duc nguoi dung, khong thay the chan doan hay don dieu tri.

## Cong nghe

4. Vi sao du an dung rule-based cho ket qua, nhung lai co AI o luong quet anh?
Goi y: Rule-based giup giai thich ro va on dinh cho phan khuyen nghi cot loi; AI duoc dung o luong quet anh de mo rong demo nhan dien tu anh that.

5. Neu AI quet anh tra sai ket qua thi sao?
Goi y: Luon hien canh bao ket qua do AI tao, khuyen nguoi dung doi chieu va chuyen sang chuyen gia neu co nguy co; khong coi day la ket luan y khoa.

6. Lam sao dam bao du lieu trong rule engine co co so?
Goi y: Repo da tach database va logic ro rang; nhom Hoa, Sinh, Nong san co vai tro tham dinh va cap nhat noi dung.

7. Prototype hien tai da chay den dau?
Goi y: Da co frontend, backend, dang nhap JWT, SQLite, quet anh, lich su scan, roadmap, check-in, streak va dat lich chuyen gia demo.

## Phap ly va dao duc

8. Du an xu ly du lieu suc khoe va anh khuon mat nhu the nao?
Goi y: Co consent truoc khi xu ly du lieu nhay cam, du lieu luu co kiem soat, co quyen xoa, va can tiep tuc hoan thien chinh sach bao mat/deu khoan su dung.

9. Neu nguoi dung tin tuyet doi vao ket qua va tu y bo qua tu van y te thi sao?
Goi y: Canh bao ro pham vi su dung, khong dua ra chan doan, va dieu huong sang chuyen gia khi van de vuot qua muc tu cham soc.

10. Co rui ro nao khi ket hop du lieu da va dinh duong tren cung ho so?
Goi y: Rui ro la do nhay cam du lieu tang len; doi lai gia tri ca nhan hoa cung tang. Do do can toi uu xin consent, phan quyen va giai thich muc dich xu ly du lieu.

## Thi truong va kinh doanh

11. Thi truong du lon de xay thanh startup khong?
Goi y: Cau tra loi can dua tren WS1; hien tai nhom chi nen khang dinh day la nhom nguoi dung dong dao va san sang thu cong cu ca nhan hoa, sau do dua so lieu.

12. Nguon thu kha thi nhat trong 12 thang dau la gi?
Goi y: Nen chon 1 huong chinh de tra loi, vi du freemium + goi scan/giai thich nang cao, con affiliate la huong mo rong sau.

13. Vi sao nguoi dung phai tra tien khi tren mang da co nhieu noi dung mien phi?
Goi y: Noi dung mien phi thuong chung chung; gia tri tra phi nam o ca nhan hoa, lich su, roadmap, theo doi va dieu huong chuyen gia.

14. Lam sao co du lieu du de thuyet phuc doi tac B2B?
Goi y: B2B la giai doan sau, chi trien khai khi co du luong nguoi dung va du lieu da an danh hoa hop le.

## To chuc thuc hien

15. Doi ngu lien nganh phoi hop voi nhau nhu the nao?
Goi y: IT xay san pham, Luat ra soat khung phap ly, Hoa/Sinh/Nong san tham dinh noi dung, cung tao loi the ma nhom thuan ky thuat thuong thieu.

16. Trong 2-3 thang toi, nhom uu tien gi?
Goi y: Hoan thien phap ly toi thieu, kiem chung nhu cau nguoi dung, chot mo hinh thu phi, va pilot voi nhom nho.

17. Neu phai cat bot pham vi san pham de chay nhanh hon, cat gi truoc?
Goi y: Cat cac huong B2B va cong dong truoc; giu profile, goi y cot loi, roadmap, check-in va luong chuyen gia.

## Rui ro

18. Rui ro lon nhat cua du an luc nay la gi?
Goi y: Chua co so lieu thi truong va tai chinh duoc xac nhan; rui ro phap ly va do tin cay noi dung can tiep tuc duoc tham dinh.

19. Phan nao cua prototype de gay hieu nham nhat voi giam khao?
Goi y: Luong AI scan anh that co the bi nham la chan doan; can noi ro day la tu dong goi y, khong phai ket luan y khoa.

20. Neu khong co internet hoac khong co API key thi demo co chay duoc khong?
Goi y: Co. Frontend va luong tim kiem thu cong van chay doc lap, quet anh that chi la mot tang demo mo rong.
