# WS2 - Draft SBMC cho DA DUONG

> Ban nay chi dung du lieu that trong repo. Cho danh dau `[CAN XAC NHAN]` la noi can nguoi trong nhom
> quyet dinh cuoi truoc khi dua vao file nop bai.

## 1. Van de xa hoi

Nguoi tre thuong chon sai san pham cham soc da va thuc pham so voi co dia rieng. He qua la ton chi phi
 thu-sai, kich ung, noi mun, che do an khong phu hop va bo di nhieu san pham dang do. Thong tin tren
 mang xa hoi thuong chung chung, kho quy doi thanh khuyen nghi ca nhan.

## 2. Doi tuong thu huong

- Nhom chinh: nguoi dung 18-35 tuoi quan tam cham soc da va dinh duong ca nhan hoa.
- Nhom thu huong truc tiep khac: nguoi co da nhay cam, di ung thuc pham, benh ly nen, nguoi muon xay
  dung thoi quen cham soc deu dan.
- Nhom thu huong gian tiep: chuyen gia da lieu/dinh duong can mot cong cu tiep can nguoi dung de hon,
  nguoi san xuat nong san noi dia neu du an mo rong theo huong goi y nguyen lieu phu hop co dia.

## 3. Gia tri de xuat

DA DUONG la nen tang ca nhan hoa cham soc da va dinh duong dua tren mot ho so co dia dung chung.
Nguoi dung nhap loai da, di ung, benh ly nen, muc tieu; he thong tra ve goi y "phu hop / can can nhac /
nen tranh" cho ca my pham va thuc pham. Ban hien co cua repo da co:

- frontend React/Vite co the chay doc lap o che do demo
- backend Express + SQLite cho dang ky/dang nhap JWT, dong bo ho so, luu lich su
- quet anh that bang Gemini API cho luong scan
- roadmap 14 ngay, check-in hang ngay, streak
- dat lich chuyen gia va luu bao cao tu van

Gia tri khac biet cot loi: ket hop cham soc da va dinh duong tren cung mot ho so ca nhan thay vi tach
rieng tung bai toan.

## 4. Giai phap

- Ho so co dia thong nhat cho da va dinh duong
- Rule-based matching de giai thich duoc ly do phu hop/khong phu hop
- Quet anh that de demo kha nang mo rong vuot qua database tinh
- Lich su scan, lo trinh, check-in va streak de chuyen tu "goi y mot lan" sang "dong hanh"
- Dat lich chuyen gia de ket noi sang tu van con nguoi khi nhu cau vuot qua pham vi tu phuc vu

## 5. Kenh tiep can

- Pitch, video demo, landing page web
- Truyen thong trong truong dai hoc, CLB, cong dong sinh vien
- Noi dung mang xa hoi ve cham soc da va dinh duong co trach nhiem
- [CAN XAC NHAN] doi tac cong dong, CLB suc khoe, CLB khoi nghiep neu nhom da co

## 6. Quan he khach hang

- Tu phuc vu qua web app
- Ca nhan hoa bang ho so va roadmap
- Ho tro nang cao qua giai thich bang AI va chat AI
- Chuyen tu online sang tu van chuyen gia khi can

## 7. Nguon thu

- [CAN XAC NHAN] Freemium: mien phi co gioi han so luot scan/tu van; goi tra phi mo rong lich su, roadmap,
  giai thich sau hon, so luot scan cao hon
- [CAN XAC NHAN] Affiliate voi san pham/phuong an duoc danh dau phu hop
- [CAN XAC NHAN] Phi dat lich/ket noi chuyen gia
- [CAN XAC NHAN] Goi insight an danh cho doi tac B2B trong giai doan sau

Khuyen nghi de pitch: chon 1 nguon thu chinh va 1 nguon thu phu, tranh trinh bay qua dan trai.

## 8. Co cau chi phi

- Phat trien va bao tri frontend/backend
- Hosting frontend va backend
- API cost cho Gemini o luong quet/giai thich/chat
- Tao noi dung, ho tro nguoi dung, review chuyen gia
- [CAN XAC NHAN] chi phi tiep can doi tac va truyen thong

## 9. Doi tac chinh

- Chuyen gia da lieu/dinh duong phuc vu luong dat lich
- Nen tang AI cloud cho quet anh va giai thich
- Don vi ha tang host backend/frontend
- [CAN XAC NHAN] doi tac san pham, nong san, thuong mai dien tu neu co

## 10. Hoat dong chinh

- Duy tri va nang cap matching engine, profile, roadmap, check-in
- Quan tri du lieu san pham/thuc pham va giai thich
- Van hanh he thong tai khoan, quet anh, lich su scan
- Kiem duyet noi dung/dieu huong nguoi dung sang chuyen gia khi can
- Thu thap phan hoi de toi uu trai nghiem va do tac dong

## 11. Nguon luc chinh

- Codebase da co frontend + backend
- Database JSON va SQLite
- Nhom lien nganh IT, luat, hoa, sinh, nong san
- Quy trinh ho so co dia va logic danh gia da ton tai trong repo

## 12. Tac dong xa hoi mong muon tao ra

- Giam mua sai va dung sai san pham/thanh phan khong hop co dia
- Giam ton that do thu-sai va giam lang phi san pham bo do
- Nang kha nang tu quan ly thoi quen cham soc da va dinh duong
- Tang kha nang tiep can khuyen nghi ca nhan hoa cho nhom tre

## 13. KPI tac dong de do luong

- Ty le nguoi dung bao cao "de hieu hon ve do phu hop cua san pham/thuc pham" sau 2 tuan
- Ty le nguoi dung bao cao giam mua nham/mua thu-sai sau 1 thang
- So nguoi dung tao roadmap va duy tri streak toi thieu 7 ngay
- Ty le nguoi dung duoc dieu huong sang chuyen gia khi gap van de ngoai pham vi tu phuc vu
- [CAN XAC NHAN] chi so lien quan den nong san/noi dia neu nhom quyet dua vao cau chuyen ben vung

## 14. SDGs va ben vung

- SDG 3: Suc khoe va cuoc song tot hon thong qua thoi quen cham soc va dinh duong phu hop hon
- SDG 12: Tieu dung co trach nhiem, giam lang phi do chon sai san pham
- Huong mo rong: goi y thuc pham/nguyen lieu phu hop co dia tu nguon trong nuoc de tang nhan dien san vat
  noi dia [CAN XAC NHAN bang du lieu va cau chuyen that]

## 15. Ranh gioi va canh bao

- DA DUONG hien la cong cu ho tro ra quyet dinh, khong phai chan doan y khoa
- Quet anh va giai thich AI co the sai; can disclaimer ro rang
- Du lieu suc khoe va anh khuon mat la du lieu nhay cam, can consent, bao mat va co quyen xoa
